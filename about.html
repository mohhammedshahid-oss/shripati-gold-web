About Shripati Gold

A family–run jewellery house combining traditional craftsmanship with modern design sensibilities.

Our Story

Shripati Gold started as a small workshop with a simple idea: create gold jewellery that our own families would be proud to wear. Over the years, we have grown into a dedicated team of designers, artisans and customer–care specialists serving clients across India.

Every piece is crafted in BIS Hallmarked gold, with careful attention to comfort, finish and long–term wear. From daily–wear chains to intricate bridal sets, our goal is to blend timeless aesthetics with reliable workmanship.

We work closely with retailers, wholesalers and private clients, offering both ready designs and made–to–order solutions for special occasions such as weddings, anniversaries and festive gifting.

What we stand for

Honest Purity

All our jewellery is crafted using certified, hallmarked gold with transparent documentation and clear specifications.

Thoughtful Design

Our design team studies comfort, weight balance and long–term wear so that every piece feels as good as it looks.

Customer Care

We believe in relationships that last beyond a single purchase: after–sales support, resizing guidance and customisation help are always available.
